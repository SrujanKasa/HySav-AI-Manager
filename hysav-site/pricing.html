Questions you'd reasonably ask

Why is this so much cheaper than the big SaaS-management platforms?

Because it does much less, on purpose. No SSO governance, no access reviews, no compliance module, no sales team to pay for. You get spend, usage, and waste visibility — the part a small team actually needs — at a price a small team can expense without a meeting.

How does HySav know what we're using?

You connect a card feed or billing inbox to auto-detect subscriptions and charges, and connect usage where tools offer it (APIs or usage exports). Anything else you can track manually in about a minute per tool.

Is there a per-seat charge?

No. It's ₹300/month flat, and ₹100 more only when your team grows past 3 people — that's the whole price list. We think per-seat pricing for a cost-saving tool would be a little too ironic.

How do I pay?

Through Razorpay — UPI, cards, or netbanking. Billing is monthly, cancel any time from settings, effective immediately.

What happens when we outgrow 30 people?

Honestly? You'll probably want one of the bigger platforms by then — you'll have an IT or finance function, and they'll want governance features we don't build. We'll help you export everything. No hard feelings.

Can I cancel any time?

Yes — self-serve, from settings, effective immediately. A tool for catching subscriptions you can't get out of should not itself be a subscription you can't get out of.
